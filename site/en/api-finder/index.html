How is the score calculated?
Capability match (max +35)The provider supports the capabilities needed for your use case.
Free-tier type and payment (max +20)No credit card required ranks highest, followed by free models, then anything.
Latency (max +15)Based on reported RPM — critical sensitivity awards higher scores for faster endpoints.
Region preference (max +30)Matches your region filter. Neutral by default; Iran direct and VPN options are scored separately.
Language support (max +15)Providers with multilingual or English-optimized models score higher.
Penalties (0 to -30)Community gateways, session bridges, or blocked/unknown statuses reduce the score.
